What is Quantum Consciousness

❝ Quantum consciousness posits that quantum phenomena within the brain contribute to the emergence of consciousness. It proposes that processes at the quantum level, such as superposition and entanglement, may influence cognitive functions. This theory attempts to bridge the gap between the quantum realm and consciousness, exploring how the uncertainty and non-locality of quantum mechanics could relate to subjective experiences. While controversial, it offers a perspective on how the complexities of quantum physics might elucidate the enigmatic nature of consciousness, suggesting that quantum effects could play a role in shaping our awareness and understanding of the mind-body connection. ❞
❝ 量子意识理论认为大脑内的量子现象对意识的产生起到作用。它提出，量子级别的过程，如叠加和纠缠，可能影响认知功能。这一理论试图弥合量子领域与意识之间的差距，探讨量子力学的不确定性和非局域性如何与主观经验相关。尽管有争议，但它提供了一种视角，说明量子物理学的复杂性可能阐明意识的神秘本质，暗示量子效应可能在塑造我们对心灵 - 身体联系的意识和理解中发挥作用。 ❞
